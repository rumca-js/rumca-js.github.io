🎵 Title: “Junk Food Love”

[Verse 1] You click the screen, I light it up Smile so wide like I’m in love You say you feel like you know me But baby, I don’t even know me

[Pre-Chorus] This ain’t a two-way street we’re on It’s edited, scripted, and turned on But still you chase a ghost in gloss A pretty lie, at your own cost
